# Atom Bowl
Atom Bowl is the #1 best way to prepare for National Science Bowl for both middle school and high school. It contains more than 10 thousand questions along with options to AI generate your own sets using the extremely powerful Gemini 2.5 Flash. Achieve mastery in your school with Atom Bowl.

The Rapid Fire questions are taken from http://science.energy.gov/wdts/nsb/high-school/high-school-regionals/hs-rules-forms-resources/sample-science-bowl-questions/, and http://science.energy.gov/wdts/nsb/middle-school/middle-school-regionals/middle-school-resources/sample-questions/.


The different question categories include: EARTH SCIENCE, MATH, ASTRONOMY, EARTH AND SPACE SCIENCE, LIFE SCIENCE, GENERAL SCIENCE, CHEMISTRY, PHYSICS, and ENERGY
